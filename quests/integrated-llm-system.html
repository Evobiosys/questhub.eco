The Integrated LLM System

A personal AI that knows your context, respects your data, and grows with you — not against you. An assistant whose incentives are aligned to the person it serves rather than to a platform behind it.

Stage: Sprouting EvoBioSys network

The seed

A language model is most useful when it knows your context — your work, your history, the way you think. But the obvious path to that is to hand all of it to a remote service, which then holds your data, shapes the assistant’s behavior to its own ends, and improves on your inputs rather than for you. Capability and dependence arrive together.

The Integrated LLM System pulls those apart. It is a personal AI that holds your context and respects your data as a precondition, not a setting — one that grows with you over time because it is yours, and whose direction of growth is set by you rather than against you.

Why it matters

Context is leverage. An assistant that truly knows your situation can do far more — but only if that knowing does not become a hold over you. An integrated system keeps the context close, under your governance, so the AI’s growing usefulness compounds in your favor instead of accruing to someone else. This is sovereignty applied to the most intimate tool of all.

  • Knows your context — integrated with your work and history, not starting cold each time.
  • Respects your data — your information stays under your control by design.
  • Grows with you — it improves in the direction you set, aligned to you rather than a platform.

What building it out looks like

  • Decide how the system holds context — what it remembers, where that lives, and who can reach it.
  • Set the data-respect guarantees up front, so privacy is structural rather than promised.
  • Define what “grows with you’’ means concretely — how the assistant adapts and who governs that adaptation.
  • Build it on the sovereign, local-first foundations the rest of the network already favors.

Status

Sprouting. The aim is articulated — an AI that is context-aware, data-respecting, and aligned to its person — and the work is to turn those three properties into a concrete design rather than a wish. It rests on the same sovereign, local-first principles that run through the network.
