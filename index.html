What is QuestHub?

Every large vision generates a trail of adjacent ideas — things noticed along the way, problems that deserve a solution, tools that should exist, communities that could form. QuestHub is where EvoBioSys keeps these alive without pretending they are all ready for full commitment.

The garden metaphor is deliberate. A gardener does not force every seed to sprout simultaneously. Some need more sun, some need different soil, some need a different season entirely. QuestHub holds them all with care and returns to them when the conditions are right.

Each quest is tagged as an #identified-interest in Tana and tracked with enough context to pick up where it left off. When a quest matures, it graduates into a full holon. When it finds a better steward, it is gifted. Nothing is wasted.

Plant a Seed

New quests emerge organically. A conversation sparks an idea. A problem is encountered that no one seems to be solving. A pattern is noticed across multiple contexts. The seed gets tagged as an #identified-interest in Tana, given enough context to be picked up later, and planted in QuestHub.

Not every seed needs to be watered immediately. The garden thrives on patience as much as action. Some seeds wait years. The point is that they are held — named, described, and ready for the moment when conditions align and a quest begins in earnest.

Share Your Quest

QuestHub is not just one gardener’s plot — it’s an open field. If you carry a quest that resonates with the EvoBioSys vision, we want to hear about it. Your seed might find its soil here.

Submitted quests are reviewed by the EvoBioSys stewards. If there’s resonance, we’ll reach out.

Governance Consideration

QuestHub connects to broader governance questions within EvoBioSys — how resources are allocated to nascent ideas, when a quest earns dedicated attention, and how stewardship is transferred when a seed finds its best gardener elsewhere. These decisions are made with care, not urgency.
